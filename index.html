Hello! I'm Senuri Wijenayake

I am a PhD Candidate at the Human-Computer Interaction Group in University of Melbourne, Australia. My research interests lay in Social Computing and Human-Computer Interaction. During my PhD I intend to investigate how socio-psychological influences such as social conformity influence human behaviour in online settings. Please refer to my publication overview for more details.

In addition to research, I am also passionate about and enjoy teaching and mentoring students and currently work as an academic tutor at the School of Computing and Information Systems . I have previously worked as a Full Stack Developer at 99X Technology, Sri Lanka leading a team of 20+ individuals developing “Tracified” - a novel product traceability solution.

Contact me on : senuri.wijenayake [at] unimelb.edu.au
